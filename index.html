About Me

Who I am

I'm an ML Engineer and AI Systems Developer based in Seongnam-si, South Korea, currently finishing my MSc in Computer Engineering at Gachon University with a GPA of 4.38/4.50.

My work sits at the intersection of deep learning model development, agentic AI systems, and multimodal data. I've trained time-series transformers on real hospital data, built RAG-powered legal AI, developed multi-agent orchestration systems with LangGraph and CrewAI, and deployed LLMs on Android via ONNX runtime — all from scratch.

I have two manuscripts under review at Q1 journals (IEEE JBHI and Elsevier ESA) and a published conference paper at ICAC 2023. My legal AI project won National 2nd Runner-Up at the SLASSCOM INGENUITY Awards 2024 and was a VC pitch battle finalist.

Previously I built data pipelines and dashboards as a Data Analyst Intern at Dialog Axiata PLC, South Asia's largest telco. I care about building AI that works in the real world — not just in notebooks.
